Welcome to Minh's Portfolio

Hey there! I'm a full stack software engineer, super stoked about my journey through the 6-month App Academy boot camp.

College was a mixed bag—I studied Biology, Pharmaceutical Sciences, and even dove into Psychology of Cognitive Sciences. But it was programming that really caught my attention. It was tough, but I craved something more hands-on and dynamic, so I ended up at App Academy.

That boot camp was a game-changer for me. I got to do a ton of paired programming and dive deep into projects using a bunch of cool tech like JavaScript, Python, React, Redux, Next, and more.

What makes me different is my mix of science background and a knack for digging deep into problems. I'm not just about tech skills—I bring dedication, reliability, and great communication to the table. I'm super excited about the chance to bring my problem-solving and tech know-how to your team as a Full Stack Software Engineer.
