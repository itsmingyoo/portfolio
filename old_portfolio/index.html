About Me

Hello, I'm Minh, a passionate software engineer and proud graduate of the intensive 6-month App Academy boot camp. My journey into technology began early, evolving into the family's go-to problem solver.

College, where I majored in Biology and Pharmaceutical Sciences, and then Psychology of Cognitive Sciences, initially lacked the intrigue I found later in programming. While my studies were demanding, I craved a more dynamic and hands-on challenge, leading me to explore programming and ultimately enroll in App Academy.

This transformative boot camp, with its paired programming and intensive project sprints which included solo and grouped projects, propelled my growth across a diverse tech stack: JavaScript, Python, React, Redux, Express.js, Flask, SQLAlchemy, Sequelize, HTML5, and CSS3.

Beyond technical skills, App Academy helped me foster a robust foundation in learning and applying languages, frameworks, and API development. Results of this would be in my BangerSounds solo project clone of SoundCloud which required learning AWS S3 on my own.

My unique background in the sciences, coupled with a proven track record of being studious and focused, sets me apart. I bring a disciplined work ethic, reliability, and effective communication to any project. I am excited to contribute my technical expertise and problem-solving mindset to your team.
